Feedback

From: Martin Der

“Hello guys! I´m a long time user of KDE and really happy about your podcast! Keep up the good work!

I would really like to hear about your opinion on a question I have (maybe on the next show?)

I know about programing but mostly for scientific applications (matlab and a bit of python). Now I would like to get a bit into GUIs and apps. Most info I found says the easiest way to get started would be in pyQt…. I really would like to keep it open source and if possible cross-platform.

Do you agree? What are the most common/your favourite IDE’s for programming in pyQt on Kubuntu?”

Our reply (in short, besides what we said in the show): We think you should look into QT, QML, QT Creator and Kalgebra.
