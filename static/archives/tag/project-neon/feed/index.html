Neon is our shiny new nightly builds service for Amarok. Neon makes it easy to follow development of Amarok by regularly providing packages for you to install. It is set up to be usable along your stable version. Even though we try to keep your data secure, you should be aware that Neon is providing mostly unreleased and therefore untested software, which might as well eat your system. Neon is intended to be used by everyone who wants to help us to find bugs, keep track of development, join development or just wants to live on the bleeding edge. It is not intended to be used by someone who needs a stable and full featured Amarok.

For more information on this service, please see the Project Neon page.
