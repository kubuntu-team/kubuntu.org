Debian 7.0 has been in development for over two years and is the product of almost a thousand volunteer developers and other contributors from around the world. We wish them many more successful releases in the years to come. Congratulations!

Debian is one of the leading Linux distributions. It is also one of the oldest
(it was started in 1993). It is the foundation on which Kubuntu is based. Thank you
Debian.
