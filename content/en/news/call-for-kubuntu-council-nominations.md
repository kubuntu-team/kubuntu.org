Hello everyone in the Kubuntu community!

We are reaching out with an exciting opportunity: we're holding an election for the Kubuntu Council, and are actively 
looking for passionate individuals to step forward as candidates.
The Kubuntu Council plays a vital role in guiding our project forward. As a community-driven flavor built on Ubuntu's
stable foundation, we deliver the latest stable KDE Plasma desktop experience to millions worldwide. The Council helps
shape strategic decisions, supports membership processes, coordinates with KDE and Ubuntu, and ensures Kubuntu remains
innovative, inclusive, and true to our open source roots.

Right now, we need fresh voices and dedicated contributors to join us—especially as we continue pushing Plasma 
advancements, improving packaging, enhancing user experience, and growing our global ecosystem.
Your involvement can directly influence the future of Kubuntu: from release planning and feature priorities to community
outreach, documentation, and long-term sustainability.

If you're an existing Kubuntu Member, we'd love to see you stand for election!

How to nominate yourself:

Create a new 'Kubuntu Council Nomination for <your name>' under the [Kubuntu Council Nomination 2026](https://linktofollow.com) topic in the Kubuntu
category on Ubuntu Discourse. Ensure to include your Launchpad ID (e.g., https://launchpad.net/~your-username).

Write a short, engaging presentation about yourself: why you'd like to stand for the Council, your background/contributions
to Kubuntu/Ubuntu/KDE/open source, and how you believe you can help shape and strengthen our project's future.

This is your chance to help steer Kubuntu toward even greater heights—making sure we keep delivering a polished, 
powerful, and free KDE experience that users love.

Nominations are open—don't hold back if you care about Kubuntu's direction! The community thrives when more of us get
involved.

Questions? Feel free to ask here, on Discourse, or in our Matrix rooms [#kubuntu:matrix.org](https://matrix.to/#/#kubuntu-discuss:ubuntu.com).

Looking forward to seeing some fantastic candidates step up!

Nominations close on 11:59pm Sunday 19th April 2026, elections to follow shortly after.
