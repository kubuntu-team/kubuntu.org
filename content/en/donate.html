Improve Kubuntu With a Donation

Your donation supports ongoing development, enhancements, and community activities. We use the funds to pay developers, cover infrastructure costs, and promote wider use of Kubuntu at conferences and other events. Just use the form at the top of the page to help make a difference.

Purchase a Kubuntu Focus System

Looking for a Linux system validated, optimized, and supported by a rich ecosystem? Kubuntu Focus offers validated Kubuntu LTS Linux systems with additional, powerful tools to help you make the most of your hardware.

Kubuntu Focus donates substantial engineering, testing, stipends, computers, and infrastructure to Kubuntu. They are also a KDE corporate patron. Your purchase of a Kubuntu Focus laptop or mini PC helps fund all of this, and enriches the entire Kubuntu ecosystem.
