# Community

Join the Kubuntu journey or get some help!

## Matrix

See the [Matrix page](/community/matrix) for more information.

## Web Forums

- [Kubuntu Web Forums](https://www.kubuntuforums.net/forum) are the best place for Kubuntu support related issues.
- [KDE Forums](https://forum.kde.org/) gives help on applications and the desktop used in Kubuntu.
- [Ask Ubuntu](https://askubuntu.com/) has a wealth of knowledge and friendly people willing to help you with your questions.
- [Kubuntu Discourse](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/c/flavors/kubuntu/187) is a discussion space for Kubuntu within the Ubuntu community.
